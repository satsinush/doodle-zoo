# Doodle Aquarium

Doodle Aquarium is a fun browser extension that lets you draw your own custom fish and watch them swim across your current webpage. The fish bounce off the screen edges and actively avoid your mouse cursor!

## Features
- **Draw Fish:** Use the extension popup to draw your own custom fish. Adjust brush size and color.
- **Manage Fish:** Keep a collection of drawn fish. You can toggle them in or out of the aquarium, or delete them.
- **Interactive:** Active fish swim around the webpage, bounce off the edges, and swim away when your mouse gets too close.

## How to Install and Test Locally in Google Chrome

Since this extension is not published to the Chrome Web Store, you can load it as an "unpacked extension" directly from the source code.

1. **Open the Extensions Page:**
   - In Google Chrome, type `chrome://extensions/` in the address bar and press **Enter**.
   - Alternatively, click the puzzle piece icon next to your profile avatar and select **Manage Extensions**.

2. **Enable Developer Mode:**
   - In the top-right corner of the Extensions page, toggle the **Developer mode** switch to **ON**.

3. **Load the Unpacked Extension:**
   - Click the **Load unpacked** button that appears in the top-left corner.
   - A file dialog will appear. Navigate to the `doodle-aquarium` folder and select it.

4. **Use the Extension:**
   - The Doodle Aquarium icon (a blue square) should now appear in your browser toolbar or under the extensions puzzle piece.
   - Click the icon to open the drawing canvas.
   - Draw a fish and click **Save Fish**.
   - Navigate to any normal webpage (Note: Chrome extensions cannot run on special pages like `chrome://newtab/` or the Chrome Web Store).
   - Watch your fish swim around! You can draw more fish and toggle their visibility from the popup.
