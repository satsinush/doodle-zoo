# Privacy Policy for Doodle Aquarium

Effective date: April 9, 2026

Doodle Aquarium is a browser extension that lets users draw fish and display them as an animated overlay on web pages.

## Summary

- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not collect analytics or tracking data.
- We do not transmit your drawings or settings to a remote server.
- User data is stored locally in your browser using extension storage.

## Information We Process

Doodle Aquarium stores the following data locally on your device:

- Fish drawings you create (image data)
- Fish state (such as enabled/disabled)
- Fish direction preferences
- Aquarium settings (such as speed and size)

This data is used only to provide the extension's core features.

## Permissions and Why They Are Needed

### `storage`
Used to save your fish drawings and settings so they persist between sessions.

### Host access on `<all_urls>`
Used so the aquarium overlay can appear on pages you visit.
The extension uses page access only to render and animate your fish overlay and respond to pointer movement for fish behavior.
The extension is not designed to read page content for profiling, ad targeting, or analytics.

## Data Sharing

Doodle Aquarium does not share your data with third parties.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your extension data remains in local browser storage until you:

- delete individual fish/settings in the extension, or
- remove the extension/clear browser extension data.

## Children's Privacy

Doodle Aquarium is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Security

We use browser-provided local extension storage and do not operate a backend for user data.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in this file with a revised effective date.
